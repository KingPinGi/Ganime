Exciting genres for the curious

You're going to fall in love with at least one genre

First, anime isn't directed solely at children like American cartoons tend to be. In Japan, you'll find anime for every age group, including adults. Since anime is directed at all ages, its content goes well beyond the child-focused themes of American cartoons. You'll find anime that features epic storylines for a wide range of interests, from comedy and romance to action and science fiction.

Expercience animations mixed with emotions like never before

If you watch modern anime, you'll quickly pick up on the unique look and feel of the anime art style. Bright, colorful graphics combine with dynamic characters and spectacular storylines to create art that has become an international phenomenon over the past thirty or more years.
